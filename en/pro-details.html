About The Property

Trust Point Real Estate is pleased to offer you this One Bedroom Apartment for SALE located in the prestigious Elite Residence, Dubai Marina.

This 1 bedroom apartment is located on a High floor. The apartment has a master with ensuite bedroom and attached balcony, separate guest washroom, storage place and fully fitted open kitchen. The apartment is currently tenanted which makes a great investment opportunity. Contact to arrange a viewing.

Property Features:

- 3 Min walk to Tram

Indoor facilities include male and female change rooms with locker areas, showers, bathrooms, Jacuzzis, sauna, steam room and two swimming pools.
The gymnasium is well equipped with extensive Life Fitness cardio and weight training gear. The gym enjoys views out over the Mina Seyahi beachfront and Palm Jumeirah. In the adjacent games room family and friends can use the pool table, air hockey, table-tennis & foot ball games.

Outside is a small children’s play area with extensive play equipment, a grassed terrace, and communal BBQ area and outdoor seating, all opening directly on to the pool deck.

Finance is available on this property through Trust Point Real Estate Mortgage partners.
